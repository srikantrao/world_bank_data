B.  Project Objectives [from section 2 of PCN] 
The objective of the proposed project is to improve the administration, delivery, financial 
sustainability, and targeting of social services through implementation of new systems and the 
adoption of new policies in the National Social Security Fund (NSSF), Ministry of Public Health 
(MOPH), Ministry of Social Affairs (MOSA)/Presidency of the Council of Ministers (PCM), and 
the Ministry of Education and Higher Education (MEHE).   
 
C.  Project Description [from section 3 of PCN] 
The proposed project will provide implementation support and capacity building to the NSSF, 
MOPH, MOSA, PCM, and MEHE over a three-year period. It will build on the achievements of 
the first Emergency Social Protection Implementation Support Project (ESPISP I), and the 
Education Development Project (EDP).
