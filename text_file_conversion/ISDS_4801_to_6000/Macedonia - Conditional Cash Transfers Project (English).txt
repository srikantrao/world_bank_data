2. Project Objectives 
The proposed project development objectives are to strengthen the effectiveness and 
efficiency of the Former Republic of Macedonia’s social safety net through (a) the 
introduction of conditional cash transfers; and (b) improvements in the administration, 
oversight, monitoring, and evaluation of social assistance transfers.   
 
3. Project Description 
Component 1 - Enhancing the Human Capital Links to Cash Assistance.  This component 
will support the implementation of a conditional cash transfer (CCT) program for poor 
families with children in secondary education.  It will also support identification, 
development and piloting of possible extensions to the CCT model, in health, labor 
and/or other levels of education.  
 
Component 2 - Strengthening Safety Net Administration.  This component seeks to 
strengthen the efficiency and effectiveness of the overall safety net – including the CCT 
and other social assistance benefits – through improved administration, service delivery, 
central and local capacity, and oversight.

Component 3 - Project Management, Monitoring and Impact Evaluation.  This 
component aims to: (i) ensure effective administration and implementation of the World 
Bank (WB) financed project; (ii) develop and put in place an effective monitoring and 
impact evaluation system; and (iii) develop and implement a public awareness campaign.
