B.  Project Objectives [from section 2 of PCN] 
•
Strengthening the controls function in SAT to minimize internal and external customs 
irregularities (such as contraband and under-valuation)  
 
•
Increasing border security  
 
•
Achieving reduction in costs for citizens and government, and improvement in processing 
times  
 
•
Contributing to improved performance of customs personnel through professionalization 
of customs staff and strengthened link between pay and performance   
 
C.  Project Description [from section 3 of PCN] 
The project would have two major components and a project management component. The first 
one will be related to the institutional redesign, and the second to the human capital 
management. The project will complement parallel activities by Customs in the areas of software 
development, information technology and communications infrastructure strengthening, review 
and simplification of the customs legal and regulatory framework, improved cooperation with 
the private sector, joint work and exchange of information with customs administrations abroad.
