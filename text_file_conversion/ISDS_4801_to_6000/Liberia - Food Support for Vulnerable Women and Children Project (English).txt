2. Project Objectives 
The
 
project development objective of this component is to 
maintain access to food 
among vulnerable households
.
3. Project Description 
1. 
This aims to reduce the food vulnerability of women and children from poor 
counties targeted by the program. The component would finance: (i) school feeding on a 
Page  2
daily basis to pre-school and primary school children; (ii) take home rations for girls in 
grade 4 to 6, as an additional incentive to encourage greater attendance by girls, (iii) 
rations for pregnant and lactating women (PLW) attending clinics and hospitals; and (iv) 
the administrative cost of the World Food Program (WFP) and its partners to administer 
the program including monitoring and evaluation of the effectiveness of the program. 
 
4. Project Location and salient physical characteristics relevant to the safeguard 
analysis 
This project will be implemented in the five counties of Maryland, Sinoe, Grand Kru, 
Grand Gedeh, and River Gee in the poorer and more remote south-east areas of Liberia.
