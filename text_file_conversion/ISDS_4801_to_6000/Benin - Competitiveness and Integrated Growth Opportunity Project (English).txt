                                                                                                  Public    3. Project Description
                                                                                                            The project will be conducted under an integrated growth sector approach. It will
                                                                                                            improve the investment climate, and the regulatory and institutional framework for
                                                                                                            infrastructure development. Concurrently the project will strengthen the clusters and

value chains in the agriculture and fishery sectors, while improving efficiency of the
underlying transit and trade systems.
 Therefore, the project will include the following components: (i) Improve business
development infrastructure through structural reforms; (b) Products and Markets
Development, and (c) Support for Project Implementation.

II. Key Safeguard Policy Issues and Their Management

A. Summary of Key Safeguard Issues

1. Describe any safeguard issues and impacts associated with the proposed project.
Identify and describe any potential large scale, significant and/or irreversible impacts:
The project is rated Category C. As designed, the project triggers no safeguard policy.
Involuntary land acquisition is not anticipated in this project. If land acquisition was
required, the project classification will shift to a B category and a resettlement policy
framework acceptable to the Bank will be put in place and disclosed in country and at the
Bank Infoshop.

 Business plans financed by the Project Development Fund (PDF) will include social and
environment impact assessments as per the Bank guidelines. In cases where the private
investment contemplated actually triggers safeguard policies, this specific project will be
rated accordingly and the same types of environment assessments the Bank used for its
directly funded projects will be applied and Government will disclose the prepared
safeguards instruments in the national media and the Bank will disclose it at the Bank
Infoshop.
