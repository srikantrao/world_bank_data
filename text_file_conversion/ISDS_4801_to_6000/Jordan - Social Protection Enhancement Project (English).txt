2. Project Objectives 
Project development objective is to improve the management and operations of the cash 
social assistance programs and to improve the access to and quality of social care 
services.   
 
3. Project Description 
The proposed project will be implemented through four components: (i) institutional 
strengthening and capacity building for development and implementation of social safety 
net programs; (ii) renewal of the National Aid Fund (NAF); (iii) improving quality, 
access to and relevance of social work and care services; and (iv) project management.  
The project will run from 2008 until 2013. The overall budget for SPEP is estimated at 
US$13.71 million, of which the IBRD will finance US$11.92 million.
