2. Project Objectives 
The project aims to: a) increase taxpayer compliance by increasing the efficiency and 
effectiveness of the DGT; b) improve good governance in tax administration by 
strengthening transparency and accountability mechanisms.   
 
3. Project Description 
The project has four components:  
 
A.Increasing the efficiency of taxpayer data collection and management. Activities will 
improve taxpayer registration, returns processing, accounts, documents management, and 
ICT infrastructure.  
 
B.Human Resource Management and Development. Activities will improve human 
resource policies, training capacity and delivery and will strengthening anti-corruption.  
 
C. Compliance management. Activities will strengthen tax audit and tax collection 
functions.  
Page  2
D. Project Management. Activities include project management, change management 
and quality control.   
 
4. Project Location and salient physical characteristics relevant to the safeguard 
analysis 
The project will finance consulting services, ICT hardware and training. There are no 
constructions or civil works under the project or any other activity that would have 
environmental implications. There are also no social safeguards implications (involuntary 
resettlement, indigenous peoples) since there will be no requirements for land acquisition. 
The Project will not prevent access to source of livelihoods for anyone.
