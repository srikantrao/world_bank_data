2. Project Objectives 
The objective of the Education for All Project is to establish a sustainable framework for 
good quality basic education. The project has three components: (i) expanding access to 
education; (ii) improving quality of education; and (iii) strengthening capacity for 
decentralized management.   
 
3. Project Description 
The additional financing would complement Component 3 by contributing to an 
uninterrupted delivery of education service by strengthening the overall human resource 
and budgetary management. The US$10 million additional financing would contribute to 
the financing of teacher salaries and incentives over a period of two months (November-
December 2008).  The financing of these recurrent expenditures is necessary to meet the 
development objectives of the project. Guinea’s currentCountry Financing Parameters 
note that there is no country limit for recurrent costs (OP 6.00).
