I A.2. Project Objectives (From PDS)I
The objective of the Project is to selectively support the Government of Afghanistan's emergency
education program Specifically, the Project will support: (a) increasing education opportunities for under-
served groups; (b) education system management reform at all levels; and (c) the introduction of modern
information technologies
I.A.3. Project Description (From PDS)
The Project will: (a) support women and girls who have not been able to access education opportunities
over recent years; (b) develop capacities to enable the effective design and management of the primary,
secondary and tertiary education sub-sectors; and (c) help strengthen the capacity of the Afghanistan
Assistance Coordination Authority (AACA) to implement and momtor reconstruction, through the use of
information and communications technology to accelerate planning, implementation and monitoring of the
reconstruction process.
