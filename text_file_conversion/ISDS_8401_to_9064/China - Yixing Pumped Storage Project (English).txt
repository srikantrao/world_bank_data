I.A.2. Project Objectives:
Increase overall system efficiency of the power sector in Jiangsu province through designing and
implementing a far reaching restructuring of the sector and furthering of competition.
I.A.3. Project Description:
Components:
1. Yixing Pumped Storage Power Plant
The development consists of: (a) a new upper reservoir impounded by a main dam with a maximum
height of 75 m and a subsidiary dam with a maximum height of 35 m, (b) expansion of an existing lower
reservoir by heightening the dam to a maximum of 50 m; (c) a water conveyance system, including
intakes and outlets, headrace and tailrace
tunnels, and surge shafts, with the total length of about 2900 m; (d) an underground powerhouse with an
installed capacity of 4 x 250 MW, (d) implementation of an environment management plan, (e)
implementation of a resettlement action plan including resettlement of 45 project affected households,
and (f) consulting services to assist in engineering, design, procurement and construction management
2.    Transmission Line Component including a 500 kV transmission line of about 25 km to connect
the power plant to the main grid
3. Power Sector Restructuring Component including further unbundling of generation from transmission,
restructuring of the generation business, commercialization of the distribution business, design and
implementation of a competitive power market, development and installation of a new IT system, further
rationalization of tariff system (efficient transmission and distribution pricing, rational consumer tariff

II.D.4. Identify the key stakeholders and describe the mechanisms for consultation and disclosure on

The preparation of the project has involved and will continue to involve all major stakeholders in project
design and implementation. The major stakeholders are:
* JPEPC is the beneficiary of the project.
* State Power Corporation (SP) and the State Development Planning Commission (SDPC) have been
involved in project design, financing and' approval. The State Environmental Protection
Administration (SEPA) has approved the environmental impact assessment report. SDPC, SP and
State Economic and Trade Commission (SETC), which has responsibility for power sector
regulation and market reform, are being consulted on sector policy and reform issues.
* Jiangsu provincial government is fully involved in project approval, the ongoing power sector
restructuring and tariff reform
* The local communities and affected people have been involved in detailed discussions related to
resettlement and environmental issues. Their concerns about the inadequate resettlement planning
and compensation will be taken into consideration fully in revising the RAP.
