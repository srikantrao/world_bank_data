L.A 2 ProjectObjectnes(FromPDS)
The objecnive of the proposed credit is to consolidate and take the reforms in the financial sector beyond
achievements to date to foster better engagement of the financial sector in the development of the Albanian
economy. The reform actions sought under the proposed credit would increase the soundness of the
banking sector and strengthen its role kn mobilizing savings and extending credit for productive activities.
I.A.3. Project Descnpnon (From PDS)
The proposed program will consist of three broad components: (i) continued reform of the banking sector
including the privatization of the Savings Bank and further strengthening of the banking regulation and
supervision. (ii) enhancement of the bankruptcy and debt resolution framework, and (iii) reform ot the non-
bank financial sector, including further development of the regulatory and supervisory franework for the
insurance sector and the privatization of INSIG.
