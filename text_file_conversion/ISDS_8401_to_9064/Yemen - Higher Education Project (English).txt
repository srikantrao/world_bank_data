I.A.2. Project Objectives:
To assist Government of Yemen to prepare a higher education reform
strategy and to pilot testing initial phases of its implementation.
I.A.3. Project Description:
The project components are: (i) good governance and accountability; (ii)
quality and relevancy; and (iii) sound financial management.
I.A.4. Project Location: (Geographic location, information about the key
environmental and social characteristics of the area and population likely
to be affected, and proximity to any protected areas, or sites or critical
natural habitats, or any other culturally or socially sensitive areas.)
Mostly concentrating on improving capacity at the Ministry of Higher
Education and Scientific Research and selected universities in Sana'a and
Aden.
