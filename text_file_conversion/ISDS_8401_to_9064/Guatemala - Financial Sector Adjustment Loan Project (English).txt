I.A.2. Project Objectives (From PDS) To support Guatemala's financial sector reform program, aimed at
bringing about a stronger and better functioning financial sector, able to withstand external shocks and to
increase prudent lending to the private sector, opening access to groups and enterprises today largely
excluded from bank financing.
I.A.3. Project Description (From PDS) The adjustment loan would support Guatemala's the financial sector
reform program, which involves the strengthening of the financial legal and regulatory framework through
the enactFnent of five financial laws (Central Bank Law, Monetary Law, Banking Law, Bank Supervision
Law and Money Laundering Law), and the regularizationlresolution of a number of weak banks.
