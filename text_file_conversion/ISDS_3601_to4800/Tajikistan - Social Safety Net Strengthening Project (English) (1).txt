B.  Project Objectives [from section 2 of PCN] 
The development objective of the proposed Project would be to establish a national registry 
system that contains accurate information on social assistance applicants and beneficiaries and 
that the Government uses to manage its social assistance programs, to budget for social 
assistance, and to monitor and oversee payments of social assistance.   
 
C.  Project Description [from section 3 of PCN] 
The first and main component would establish a national registry of social assistance.  The 
second component would train government personnel to operate and maintaine the registry. It 
would build capacity through purchases of computer equipment and through minor rehabilitation 
of offices.  It would also build the capacity of government personnel to use the registry to control 
and audit payments of social assistance.  The third and final component would be for project 
management.

The project is rated Category C, and no environmental management plan is necessary.  The 
Project will finance minor repairs of offices of the Ministry of Labor and Social Protection 
and some furniture.  The Project will not finance major rehabilitation, such as structural 
repairs or replacement of roofs. It will not finance any extension of structures beyond their 
original footprint, and it will not finance any new construction or land purchases. 
Nevertheless, the Project Operations Manual will include the safeguards procedures for civil 
works that are now applied by the Community Health Project in Tajikistan.
