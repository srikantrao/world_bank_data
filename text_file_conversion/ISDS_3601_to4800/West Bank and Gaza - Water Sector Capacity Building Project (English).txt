A. Summary of Key Safeguard Issues 
1. Describe any safeguard issues and impacts associated with the proposed project.

activities in the project area: 
The project is unlikely to have indirect or long term impacts.  The project will evaluate 
the current roles and responsilities of the Palestinian Water Authority with respect to 
implementation of Palestinian Water Law and develop a capacity building plan for the
