A.2. Project Objectives 
Project would test the effectiveness of various interventions to enhance delivery of public services by 
districts and governorates. 
 
A.3. Project Description 
Service delivery capacity building
.  Strengthen management information systems of Ministry of Local 
Administration (MOLA) and local authorities; help districts develop and implement adequate operations 
and maintenance budgets, prioritized investment programs incorporating all sources of financing, 
participatory development plans, and good accounting and procurement capacities; assist governorates in 
building economic development strategies and public-private partnerships for development; and prepare a 
national strategy for implementation of a decentralization. 
Local revenue
.  Provide technical support for tax policy and administration reforms to improve local 
revenue generation, training in local revenue collection, and a system of fiscal transfers that rewards 
districts for good planning, programming, budgeting and budget execution, and fiduciary practices. 
Project management and monitoring and evaluation
.  Technical support to credit management; and 
monitoring and evaluation to measure impact of project activities.  
 
A.4. Project Location and salient physical characteristics relevant to the safeguard analysis: 
Project activities would take place in Sana’a (MOLA headquarters) and 20 districts in four governorates, as 
well as the four governorate headquarters.
