                                                                                                            B. Project Objectives [from section 2 of PCN]
                                                                                                            The main development objective of the proposed project is to promote growth through
                                                                                                            developing least-cost power generation for domestic use in an environmentally sustainable and
                                                                                                            efficient manner. In addition to mobilizing private capital, the proposed project would
                                                                                                            encourage private sector ownership and management of the power sector, and sector reform.

                                                                                                             The project?s impacts would be increasing electricity sales (GWh) and revenues (including
                                                                                                            fiscal/tax revenues), and expanding access to electricity (through an increase in number of
                                                                                                            consumers and in per capita consumption).
