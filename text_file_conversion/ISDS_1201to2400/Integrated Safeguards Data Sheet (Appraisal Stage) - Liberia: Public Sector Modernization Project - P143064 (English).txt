                         3. Project Description
                         The Project Development Objective (PDO) is improved performance, pay and payroll management
                         in the Civil Service in Liberia.
Public Disclosure Copy




                         Component 1: Improved Pay Management
                         The objectives of this component are (i) to provide Civil Service compensation that can attract and
                         retain qualified and skilled personnel; (ii) to facilitate sustainable wage bill management, and (iii)
                         provide a clear and fair basis for accountability at the agent level. The project will support the
                         following activities: (i) undertake a mapping of current pay structure; (ii) conduct comparative
                         remuneration survey;(iii)Assign civil servants to relevant grades;(iv) revise HR policy manual to
                         include pay administration and grading procedures;(v) train CSA staff in key HR functions;(vi)
                         revise of pay strategy; (vii) conduct information awareness campaign.


                         Component 2: Strengthened Payroll Management

                         The objective of this component is (a) to establish and maintain a clean, accurate and complete Civil
                         Service payroll and (b) ensure predictability in the governmentâ€™s wage bill through the frequent
                         monitoring of payroll exits and entries. The project will support the following activities: (i)
                         preparation and dissemination of HR planning and procedures; (ii) strengthening of personnel
                         records and issuance of biometric cards; (iii) decentralization of HRMIS in at least four CSA
                         outreach centers ; (iv) independent systems audit by GAC; (v) train ministry staff in payroll
                         management functions; (vi) and strengthen payroll controls;

                         Component 3: Improved Performance

                         The objective of this component is to (i) support the ministries and agencies to focus on performing
                         their core functions. It will support the strengthening of managerial, professional and technical
Public Disclosure Copy




                         capacity in MACs to ensure that agents in organizations can perform functions and deliver on the
                         mandate of the ministries; (ii) establish performance and accountability standards among civil
                         servants.

                         The project will support the following activities:

                         (a)     Subcomponent 3.1 (Organizational Development): (i) mapping of existing organization
                         structures and revision of current organizational re-design model; (ii) conducting mandates, function
                         and organization review of selected ministries; (iii) undertaking organizational diagnosis of each
                         MAC (including staffing and skills gaps); (iv) preparation of legislation to amend structure and
                         functions of MACs; and (v) preparation of options for voluntary separation program for eligible civil
                         servants.

                         (b)     Subcomponent 3.2 (Human Resource Management): (i) develop a policy framework for
                         Civil Service management (including manpower planning, merit appointment; and performance
                         management); (ii) support training and career development (including of HR and line managers in
                         CSA and across ministries; (iii) develop a policy framework for establishing a Civil Service
                         Commission; and (iv) develop a policy framework for appointment of Principal Administrative
                         Officers.
