 2. Project Development Objective(s)
 Improving efficiency and transparency of the national public finance management system of Belarus.

 3. Project Description
 The project (total estimated project cost US$1.2 million, estimated implementation period 26 months) is
 envisaged to have three components:
A. Development of PFM reform strategy and action plan and support for their
implementation. Support under this component will include the necessary additional
diagnostics, consultancy to develop the PFM strategy and action plan, peer learning,
stakeholder consultations and communication activities. The component will also finance
consultancy services to support the initial phase of implementation of the approved PFM action
plan in selected priority areas;
B. Design of an Integrated Financial Management Information System . This will include
(a) the development of FMIS technical and functional design; (b) the development of tender
documentation for procurement and customization of the software and hardware; (c) technical
assistance in building capacity for managing FMIS development and implementation; (d) the
carrying out of workshops (national and international) and study visits to inform the design of
said system; and
C. Project Management Capacity Building component will finance building local capacity
for managing PFM reform and the future PFM project expected to support the reform
implementation. It will finance training and awareness building activities for the members of
the MoF PFM taskforce and the staff of the IT center. Grant resources will also be used to
finance salaries of consulting team that will be recruited to help the IT Center administer the
project, as well as to cover operating costs and grant audit.
