                            2. Project Development Objective(s)
                           The project development objective is to strengthen the financial and institutional capacity of the
                           Deposit Insurance Agency so as to meet its legal deposit insurance and bank resolution obligations.
                            3. Project Description
                           The project development objective is to strengthen the financial and institutional capacity of the
                           Deposit Insurance Agency so as to meet its deposit insurance and bank resolution obligations. This

                                                                       Page 1 of 4
                           will be accomplished through:
                           a.       capitalizing the Deposit Insurance Fund with sufficient financing to meet its deposit
                           insurance and bank resolution obligations.
Public Disclosure Copy




                           b.       ensuring that the Deposit Insurance Agency is able to manage the financial resources it has
                           available so as to meet its deposit insurance and bank resolution obligations.

                           The proposed project has two components that aim to accomplish the Project Development
                           Objective.
                           1)      A results based component of US$199 million that capitalizes the Deposit Insurance Fund
                           based on satisfactorily achieving Disbursement Linked Indicators that improve the ability of the DIA
                           to meet its mandated deposit insurance and bank resolution obligations.
                           2)      A technical assistance component of US$1 million that aims to strengthen the ability of the
                           DIA to meet its deposit insurance and bank resolution obligations and achieve the DLIs.
