2. Project Objectives
To enhance transparency and efficiency in central and sub-national public finance and
human resource management.



 To establish and consolidate an equitable resource sharing mechanism between central
and sub-national government.

3. Project Description
Component 1: Strengthening public financial management and public service
management at central government level, including establishing a functioning system of
intergovernmental fiscal relations
 Component 2: Building Public Sector and Financial Management Systems at Provincial
Level



 Component 3: Governance reform implementation capacity
