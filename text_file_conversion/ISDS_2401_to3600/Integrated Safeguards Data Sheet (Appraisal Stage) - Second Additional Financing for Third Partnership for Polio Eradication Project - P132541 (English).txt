                              2. Project Objectives

                              The project's development objective is to assist Pakistan’s efforts to eradicate Polio through procurement, supply and effective use of the oral polio
                              vaccine (OPV) in the country’s supplementary immunization activities (SIAs) during 2011-13.

                              3. Project Description

                              Project description: The project has only a single component: procurement, timely supply and effective use of oral polio vaccines OPV during SIAs.
Public Disclosure Copy




                              This component is one of the three components of the Pakistan’s Polio Eradication Initiative (PEI): (i) Procurement, supply and effective use of oral
                              Polio vaccines, (ii) supplemental immunization activities, and (iii) surveillance.
