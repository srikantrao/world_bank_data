B.  Project Objectives [from section 2 of PCN] 
The TMP’s development objective is the same as for TAP namely to assist the GoTin raising its 
tax revenues without increasing tax rates.   
 
C.  Project Description [from section 3 of PCN] 
The components of the TMP support all these activities but are designed to reflect the five (5) 
goals of Tanzania Revenue Authority;s (TRA) corporate plan. The components are summarized 
below.  
 
Component 1: Improve revenue collection in a cost effective way: narrow the revenue gap i.e. 
collect eligible revenue, improve the tax structure, and modernize customs arrangements.  
 
Component 2: Full integration of TRA operations: Integrate processes and procedures, 
strengthen Large Taxpayer’s Department; provide information technology infrastructure, 
implement the Asycuda++ system for customs; and strengthen Domestic Revenue department.  
Page  2
Component 3: Provision of high quality and responsive customer service: develop taxpayer 
education capacity, roll out district one stop centers, promulgate a taxpayer’s charter, establish 
customs client services unit.  
 
Component 4: Promote tax compliance through a fair, equitable and transparent application of 
tax laws: unify taxpayer identification, introduce risk management for audit-based taxes, 
strengthen the Tax Investigation Department and improve tax enforcement.  
 
Component 5: Improve staff competence, motivation, integrity and accountability: strengthen 
management controls, promulgate a staff charter, strengthen training capacity, and review anti-
corruption strategy.  
 
TMP will also provide support to the Zanzibar Revenue Board (ZRB) for improving efficient 
collection of taxes; promotion of voluntary compliance; provision of quality service to taxpayers; 
and improvement of working environment, staff skills, competence and motivation.   
 
D.  Project location (if known) 
The project will be located in Urban and semi Urban areas in Tanzania
