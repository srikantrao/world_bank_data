                                                                                                            2. Project Objectives
                                                                        Public                              The objectives of the project are to provide the public training system with improved
                                                                                                            mechanisms for responsiveness to employment needs, and to enhance the capacity and
                                                                                                            effectiveness of the Skills Development Fund in articulating and financing the training
                                                                                                            needs of enterprises.

                                                                                                            3. Project Description
                                                                                                            Component 1: Monitoring and Evaluation, and Project Management
                                                                              Authorized
                                                                                                             This component will provide technical assistance (TA), training, and goods to: (i)
                                                                                                            develop MTEVT?s capacity in planning, monitoring and evaluation; (ii) implement the
                                                                                                            first phase of a sector management information system; (iii) assist planning and

                                                                                                            manage implementation of the project.

                                                                                                             Component 2: Design and Delivery of Training Programs

 The central purpose of Component 2 is to pilot a series of initiatives ? at the pre-service,
in-service, and instructor training levels ? that will demonstrate the benefits to be gained
from collaborating with employers, and incorporating the expectations of employers, in
all phases of the process of occupational skills development. Activities are included in
each sub-component, designed to monitor implementation, identify constraints and
opportunities as they arise, assess the outcomes, and engage the respective MTEVT
departments in a manner that will facilitate wider replication of the innovations where
benefits have been demonstrated.

 Component 3: Reform of the Skills Development Fund

 The objective of this component of the project is to support the reform and restructuring
of the SDF under revised legislation.

4. Project Location and salient physical characteristics relevant to the safeguard
analysis
Project activities related to information management will be countrywide. The training
institutes that will pilot the new pre- and in-service activities are located in Sana'a, Aden,
Taiz and Mukalla.
