A.2. Project Objectives 
The overall objective of the project is to assist the authorities to develop the capacity to 
manage public expenditures in an effective, transparent and accountable manner in order 
to achieve satisfactory and sustainable development outcomes in Afghanistan.  The 
project will have a particular focus on enhancing the performance of public sector 
procurement, financial management, and accountability systems.   
A.3. Project Description 
In pursuit of the above project objectives, the project will finance continued technical 
assistance operational support in core fiduciary functions along with technical assistance 
for implementation of legislative, regulatory, and institutional reforms; systems 
development; and human resource development.  The project has five components and 
four implementing agencies.  
Component 1: Strengthening Procurement Capacity within Government  - Afghanistan 
Reconstruction Development Services (ARDS) and a nascent Procurement Policy Unit. 
Component 2.  Strengthening Financial Management Capacity 
–
Ministry of Finance 
Component 3:  Strengthening Internal Audit Capacity – Ministry of Finance 
Component 4:  Strengthening External Audit Capacity within Government – Control and 
Audit Office 
Component 5:  Other Institutional Strengthening and Capacity Building – Ministry of 
Finance 
 
A.4. Project Location and salient physical characteristics relevant to the safeguard 
analysis: 
The project location is Kabul, Afghanistan although some training and capacity building 
Page  2
may reach out into provincial areas.  As a technical assistance project focused on 
enhancing public administration capacity in fiduciary areas, it will not affect protected 
areas, sites or critical natural habitats, or any other culturally or socially sensitive areas.
