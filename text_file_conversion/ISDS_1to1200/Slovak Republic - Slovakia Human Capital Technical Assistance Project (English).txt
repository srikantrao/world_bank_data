B.  Project Objectives [from section 2 of PCN] 
 
The main development objective of the project therefore is to contribute to decrease in poverty and 
social exclusion, sustainability of the reforms and to the long-term prosperity of the country through 
improvements in crucial elements of the policy infrastructure in the areas of employment, education and 
social cohesion
.
By the infrastructure for policy making, is meant  human resources involved in policy-
making; management systems and processes governing policy preparation, implementation and 
monitoring; and policy outputs that set out actual policies.  
 
C.  Project Description [from section 3 of PCN] 
 
Component 1: Creation of a Policy Capacity Framework 
 
This component will provide international and national consultancy services to (i) assess the 
current institutional capacity for policy making and monitoring in the two participating Ministries; (ii) 
develop a consistent and sustainable strategy to increase technical capacity for policy making; and (iii) 
develop a set of monitorable indicators to assess the effectiveness and impact of project activities. It will 
also support the establishment of: (i) an inter-ministerial committee for coordination; and (ii) a system for 
monitoring the effectiveness of the activities undertaken under the project. 
Page  2
2
Component 2: Investment in human resources  
 
This component will provide international and national consultancy services for: (i) setting up HR 
management systems within the ministries, (ii) development of software for HR management; (iii) 
training, internships and scholarships aimed at the development and strengthening of skills and 
knowledge of civil servants at the ministries and other institutions (policy employees) needed for public 
policy
–making, (iv) organisation of seminars, conferences and other events for the key ministries of the 
project and for other relevant institutions. 
 
Component 3: Building and upgrading institutional capacity  
 
This component will provide international and national consultancy services to develop institutional 
policies and procedures for modern policy making functions to include (i) development of policy targets, 
indicators and monitoring systems, (ii) costing and budgeting, (iii) implementation, (iv) monitoring and 
evaluation; and (v) improving the quality of key policy outputs with a focus on national planning 
documents. 
 
D.  Project location (if known) 
 
The Project will be implemented at the central level of the Government in the Ministry of Labor, Social 
Affairs and Family, Ministry of Education and their related institutions. The Project is aimed at building 
staff’s capacity in policy-making through technical assistance and training provision, and will not have 
any potential impact on safeguard policies.
