B. Project Objectives
12.    This operation will support Government's PFM reforms, Financial Management Information
System (FMIS) design and FMIS implementation as a series of investment projects financed by two
IBRD credits. The project development objective (PDO) for the series is to improve efficiency and
transparency of the national public finance management system of Belarus.
13. PDO for the first operation in this series is to develop a) the regulatory framework for budget
preparation, execution, accounting and reporting and b) the specifications and implementation


﻿arrangements for a new Financial Management Information System that will support implementation
of the regulatory framework. The Second operation will focus on establishment of the new FMIS.
C. Project Description
The project is first in a series of two projects. Estimated amount of the 1st loan - USD 10 mlIn,
estimated amount of the overall series- USD 100 million. Project aims at improving enabling
environment to improve efficiency and transparency of the national PFM system of Belarus,
including supporting essential budget reforms to streamline rules and procedures for budget
a      preparation, execution, accounting and reporting and create the basis for establishment of the new
FMIS. The project is expected to improve the ability to better plan the budget in medium term
perspective, supporting establishment of internal control environment that facilitates focus on results,
and improving reliability of financial data, which is essential for managing fiscal risks and decision-
making. The project comprises of three components financing TA, change management activities and
procurement of IT. The components are improvement in budget management rules and procedures;
PFM IT solutions design and implementation, which includes preparation of bidding documents for
FMIS and procurement of debt management software; change management, which includes support
for project management, various types of capacity building and communication activities.
