3. Project Description
The proposed NHAI TA project will have one thematic component - Institutional Strengthening and
Capacity Building under which there will be Provision of technical assistance and training to enable
MoRTH and NHAI, enhance its program management and operational efficiency in the areas of,
inter alia, project preparation and management, standardization and research, asset management,
financing options, process transformation, logistics efficiency, surveying, quality audit, media
campaign for Road Safety awareness, reducing carbon footprint, highway advisory radio,
development of Model Concession Agreements for expressway and Tunneling projects, performance
monitoring of vendors, corporate governance, new contract modalities and security audit of IT
infrastructure; or any other such activities supporting the project development objectives.
4. Project location and salient physical characteristics relevant to the safeguard
analysis (if known)
It is a Technical Assistance Project involving undertaking studies and preparation/review of policies/
programs. The future activities as a result of the TA project, which may not be funded by the World
Bank, may spread all over India. However if Bank will fund any such operations, then relevant
policies will be triggered.

A. Summary of Key Safeguard Issues
1. Describe any safeguard issues and impacts associated with the Restructured project.
Identify and describe any potential large scale, significant and/or irreversible impacts:
There are no physical works involved as part of the project. Therefore, activities under this TA
project themselves do not have direct adverse environmental or social impacts, but the outcomes
of TA support i.e. implementation of the recommendations of the studies may lead to specific
projects/investments operations (which may not be funded by the Bank) having significant
environmental and social implications, entailing risks and potentially inducing adverse impacts.
