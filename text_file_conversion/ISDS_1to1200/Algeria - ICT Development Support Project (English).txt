2. Project Objectives 
The objective of the proposed project is to increase ICT access and use of electronic 
services by government, businesses and individuals.   
 
3. Project Description 
(a) Consolidate the postal and telecommunications sector reform and strengthen the ICT 
sector enabling (policy, legal, regulatory, and institutional) framework;  
 
(b) Leverage ICT adoption and use by the government, businesses, and citizens; and  
 
(c) Strengthen communication strategy and the evaluation and supervision capacity of 
the partner institutions.
