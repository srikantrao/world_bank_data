2. PROJECT INFORMATION

 Current Project Development Objective
 The overall development objective of the proposed operation is to help the GoC improve the quality of its PFM system
 to increasethe efficient and transparent use of public resources, in particular the expected oil revenue, to attain MDGs.
 To this end, theproposed IDA project will support the implementation of the Government PAMFIP action plan for
 modernization of PFM in a concertedmanner with other donors.
