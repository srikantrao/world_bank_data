B. Project Objective 
 
The objective of the GERL is to (a) increase fiscal flexibility by reducing, over time, PS 
expenditures as a proportion of total national government expenditures; (b) strengthen civil 
service establishment controls and reduce national government civil service employment; and (c) 
strengthen civil service efficiency, incentives and integrity. 
 
C. Project Description 
 
The GERL is envisaged to have three components: 
A.  
Administrative Streamlining and Establishment Control
:
this could finance (a) 
organizational restructuring and voluntary separation; (b) re-training, counseling and job search 
support; and (c) implementation of an establishment control system.  
B. 
Performance and Integrity
:
this could finance (i) fiscally responsible and transparent 
incentives for civil servants in the selected agencies; (ii) merit-based recruitment, performance 
evaluation and promotion practices to reduce political influence in appointments and promotions; 
and (iii) civil service integrity systems in the selected agencies. 
C.  
Project Governance
:
this could finance incremental implementation costs, e.g. consultations 
with employee associations and other stakeholders, a communications and public information 
campaign, civil society oversight, and project implementation.
